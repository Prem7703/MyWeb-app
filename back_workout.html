Back Workout

Pull-up

Hang using an overhand grip, and then pull your shoulder blades down towards the floor窶琶magine moving your shoulders down and away from your ears. Continue until the bar is just below your chin, and hold that position for three seconds

40s

Seated cable row

Once seated on the bench, make sure your knees are slightly bent, and keep the torso vertical. In this tall position, pull the cable towards the middle of your torso while squeezing your shoulder blades together, and slowly return to the start. Do 10 reps.

40s

Dumbbell bent-over row

stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand with a neutral grip. Bend slightly at your knees, hinge your hips, and lower your chest so that it is almost parallel to the floor. Keeping your core tight and your arms close to the body, row both weights up towards your chest, bending at the elbow.

40s

Lat Pulldown

A lat pulldown is a back exercise that targets the latissimus dorsi muscles, also known as the lats
Sets: 3 (do a failure set) Reps: 12

40s

T-Bar Row

A T-bar row is a resistance exercise that strengthens the back, core, and upper body
Sets: 3 (do a failure set) Reps: 10

40s

Dead lift

The deadlift is a full-body exercise that involves bending down to pick up a weighted object and lift it up
