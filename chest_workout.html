Chest Workout

Bench Press

The bench press is a fundamental chest exercise that targets the pectoral muscles, triceps, and shoulders. Perform 3 sets of 8-12 reps with a moderate weight.

40s

Incline Press

The incline press focuses on the upper portion of the chest, helping to build a well-rounded chest. Aim for 3 sets of 10 reps at a comfortable incline.

40s

Push-ups

Push-ups are a bodyweight exercise that works the entire chest, along with the triceps and core. Try for 4 sets of 15-20 reps to maximize endurance.

40s

Incline Dumbbell Bench Press

This variation is still hitting your upper chest muscles. But you’ll also feel your triceps and deltoids working during this movement.
Sets: 3 (do a failure set) Reps: 12

40s

Dumbbell Chest Fly

This upper chest workout requires a flat bench. We don’t want to overwork our upper chest muscles.
Sets: 3 (do a failure set) Reps: 10

40s

Dumbbell Pullover

This is a classical all-round chest exercise. The dumbbell pullover works your pectorals, triceps, serratus, and latissimus dorsi.
Sets: 3 Reps: 12

40s
