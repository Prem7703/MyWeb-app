Warm-Up Exercises

Jumping Jacks

Perform jumping jacks for 30 seconds to increase heart rate and warm up your entire body.

Arm Circles

Do 20 arm circles forward and backward to loosen up your shoulders and arms.

Leg Swings

Swing each leg forward and backward for 10 repetitions to loosen up your hip joints.

Neck Rolls

Gently roll your neck in a circular motion for 15 seconds to relieve tension.

Lunges

Perform 10 lunges on each leg to activate your lower body muscles and improve flexibility.

Toe Touches

Bend forward and reach for your toes, holding for 10 seconds to stretch your hamstrings and lower back.
